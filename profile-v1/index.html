Our Story
"It all started with a deer."
Our founder was frustrated when a deer kept sneaking into his backyard and munching on his wife's beloved vegetable garden. After setting up a camera to catch the culprit, he found that it didn't provide enough information to solve the mystery.
"When Security Meets Fun."
This lightbulb moment led to the creation of SensForge. We develop smart, AI-powered cameras that not only keep you safe but also capture wonderful everyday moments.
