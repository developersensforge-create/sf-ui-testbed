How It Works · Business Model

Simple to Play.
Strong to Monetize.

1

Parent Starts

Opens Play Mode in app, selects a game, taps Start. One-time parental consent. Kids play independently after that.

2

Camera Runs the Game

AI vision watches movement via 480p frames sent to cloud. Game narrated via camera speaker. No display, no controller.

3

Highlights Auto-Captured

Funny freeze poses, epic near-misses, first-clue I Spy wins — saved as shareable clips. Push notification to parent.
